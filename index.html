Instructions:

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four pendants below. Clicking each pendant will add a specific amount to your Current total.

You win when your Current total is the same as the Number to match. You lose the game if your Current total goes above the Number to match.

The value of each pendant is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game restarts, the value of the pendants will change again.
